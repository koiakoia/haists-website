For farms & ag operations

Your tractor has more computers than your office

Agriculture is the seventh most-targeted industry for cyberattacks in the country. Farm operations are running on connected technology now — but the office, the network, and the data management haven't kept up.

🌾

Data scattered everywhere

Deere Operations Center, Climate FieldView, Bushel, your crop insurance files, QuickBooks — none of them talk to each other. We help organize what you've got and keep it backed up.

🔒

Farm office wide open

The computer that handles payroll, grain contracts, and equipment financing probably has no backup plan and the same password as your email. That's fixable in an afternoon.

📡

One network for everything

Grain bin sensors, security cameras, the house, the shop, the office — all on one router from 2018. We separate things properly so a problem in one place doesn't take down everything.

✉

Fake invoices and wire fraud

When you're wiring $200K for seed or equipment, one spoofed email from “the dealer” can be catastrophic. Simple email protections stop this before it starts.

📋

Reporting requirements coming

Carbon credit programs, sustainability documentation, grain buyer data requests — all require organized, trustworthy records. Better to get ahead of it than scramble later.

🔧

Vendor lock-in questions

Who actually has access to your yield data? What's Deere collecting? What happens when you want to switch platforms? We help you understand what you're sharing and with whom.
